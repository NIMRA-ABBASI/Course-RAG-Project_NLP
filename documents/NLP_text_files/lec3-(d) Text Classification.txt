What is Text Classification?

Text Classification is the process of assigning a label or category to a piece of text based on its content.



Common Applications of Text Classification

Assigning subject categories or topics:
Automatically sorting news articles into categories like sports, politics, or technology.

Spam detection:
Identifying unwanted emails or messages and filtering them out.

Authorship identification:
Figuring out who wrote a particular text based on writing style.

Age/gender identification:
Predicting the age group or gender of the writer from their text.

Language identification:
Determining the language of a given text, like English, Spanish, or French.

Sentiment analysis:
Detecting if the text expresses positive, negative, or neutral feelings.
