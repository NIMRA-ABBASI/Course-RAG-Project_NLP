Who was Diwan Bahadur Singha

He was the most prominent leader of the Christian community of the subcontinent, who was born in Pasrur of district Sialkot Pakistan.

He was elected in 1937 as a member of Punjab assembly. The members include Diwan Singha, Fazal Elahi and Mr. Cecel Gibbon from the Christian community in Punjab assembly of British India.

Diwan Singha was also the speaker of the acting assembly.

Diwan Singha along with two other members decided to side with Pakistan in assembly voting.
