Pakistan as Member of SAARC

Pakistan is an active member of SAARC which provide suitable frameworks to its member states to collectively stimulate cooperation and progress and to promote peace and stability in South Asia.

Flexible foreign policy would certainly restore Pakistan’s position and prestige in the region as well as in the world.

There is also a crucial need to begin again meaningful dialogue with India in order to reach at a reasonable arrangement on all conflicting issues. Pakistan has to reach out all its neighbors particularly it has to reestablish its relations with Afghanistan, strengthen its ties with other South Asian countries.

Moreover, building new diplomatic and economic partnerships with powerful states should be foremost priority of Pakistan’s foreign policy makers.
