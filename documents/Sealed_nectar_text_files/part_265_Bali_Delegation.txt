Bali Delegation: Their arrival was in Rabi‘ Al-Awwal, the ninth year of Al-
Hijra. They embraced Islam, stayed in Madinah for three days. Their chief Abu 
Ad-Dabeeb wondered whether hospitality was rewarded by Allâh. The 
Messenger of Allâh [pbuh] said:  
"Yes, and so is any charity you offer to poor or rich people that is 
Sadaqah." 
He also inquired about the time allotted to hospitality. "Three days," said he. 
"What about the stray ewe?" The Prophet said: "It is either yours or your 
brother’s, otherwise it goes to the wolf." He inquired about the stray camel.
